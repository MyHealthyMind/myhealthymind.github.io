Information regarding crisis helplines and how to reach them can be found here.

Helplines

Crisis Text Line

Text "HOME" to 741-741

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

Call or text 988

Love Is Respect

Text "LOVEIS" to 22522 or Call 1-866-331-9474

National Eating Disorder Association

Text "NEDA" to 741741

Trevor Lifeline

Text "START" to 678-678 or Call 866-488-7386

It's Okay to Talk About Suicide (NAMI Infographic)

In a Crisis?

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline:
Call or Text 988
OR call 1-800-273-TALK (8255)

Crisis Text Line:
Text "HOME" to 741-741 to connect with a Crisis Counselor
