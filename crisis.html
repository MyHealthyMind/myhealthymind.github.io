Crisis Text Line

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

Call 1-800-273-TALK (8255)

Love Is Respect

Text "LOVEIS" to 22522 or Call 1-866-331-9474

National Eating Disorder Association

Call 800-931-2237

Trevor Lifeline

Text "START" to 678-678 or Call 866-488-7386
